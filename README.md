# TailorPilot

tailors your cv and cover letter to any job posting, right where you're applying.

no more copy-pasting your resume into chatgpt in another tab and hoping it doesn't
make stuff up about your experience. tailorpilot reads the job posting, rewrites
your cv to match it, and checks every claim it makes against your real cv before
showing it to you.

still early days, building this in public, section by section.

## status
🚧 in progress

## how it works
1. parser reads the job posting and pulls out what actually matters (skills, seniority, responsibilities)
2. writer rewrites your cv and drafts a cover letter based on that
3. grounder double-checks every line traces back to something real in your original cv — nothing gets invented

more to come as it's built.
